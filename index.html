About Us

GDSC
UST
Google Developer Students Club

is a community led by university students and powered by Google developers. The aim of this club is to help students from all disciplines to improve their skills and knowledge in technology. It gives them a chance to grow their networks using peer-to-peer learning as well as communicating with other Google developers.

A student in any GDSC has a chance to become a real developer and build great solutions for their local communities and get enormous job opportunities after graduation.

Now and for the first time, we have this great chance at our University! GDSC University of Science and Technology is the GDSC community opened for all UST students to learn, share, connect and develop their skills and increase their knowledge.

The University of Science and Technology is one of the leading Universities in Yemen and the middle east. UST offers programs that lead to bachelor's, master's, and PhD degrees in a large number of majors.

GDSC of the University of Science and Technology is welcoming any under graduated UST student who has a passion to learn and take a big step into the big world.
